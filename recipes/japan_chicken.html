Japan Chicken

Description

Japan chicken is roaming all over the internet. It is a popular dish in one of the erode restaurant called Amman mess. Once the dish got its fame, Many restaurant all over tamilnadu started adding this in their menu too.

Ingredients For Japan Chicken Recipe

  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 10 cloves Garlic chopped
  • 5 Green chillies slit
  • 2 cups Milk
  • ¼ cup Cashew Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp White pepper powder
  • 500 grams Chicken (preferably boneless)
  • 2 tbsp All Purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp Cornflour

Instructions for Making Japan Chicken

  1. Take chicken in a bowl. Add in salt, white pepper powder and mix well. Add cornflour, maida and little water and mix really well to form a thick mass. Heat oil for deep frying. Drop chicken in hot oil and fry till golden brown around 5 to 6 mins. Drain on a paper towel and set aside.
  2. Now make the sauce. Heat butter in a pan. Add in garlic and saute for few seconds. Add in green chillies and mix well. Pour in milk, cashew powder, salt sugar and white pepper. Mix well.
  3. Add in the fried chicken and coat well in the sauce. Let the chicken cook in the sauce for 3 to 4 mins. The sauce will thicken and coat the chicken.
  4. Serve hot.
